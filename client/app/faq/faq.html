Frequently Asked Questions

How do I invite someone?

Type the '@' symbol in the tag box, and then type the users name as it appears in your contact book. Once you see desired person's name, select it. Any person can be invited whether they have or do not have the app. All users have unlimited invites.

Can anyone see my event?

If the top tag at an event is private, then only invited users will be able to see it. Otherwise, it is open to all users.

What are pioneer points?

Pioneer points are a measure of ones prominence and status within the app. Proper and frequent use of the app's various features will increase one's pioneer points-- which gives the user more influence and privilege at events. More specifically, pioneer points dictate how many tags a user may select at a given event.

What is a private tag?

To create a private tag, type a '#' and then enter the tag you would like. Once the the tag been has selected, it will be magenta instead of cyan. Unless it is the top tag, private tags can only be viewed by people who were invited within the same submit on which they were upvoted. If the top tag is private, only invited users can see the event.

Why did my event disappear?

Events expire 2 hours after the last check in.

Why did the app provide the wrong address?

Sometimes the location services provided by the GPS are inaccurate. If you notice you are tagging an incorrect address, you can click the refresh icon at the bottom right corner of the screen.
